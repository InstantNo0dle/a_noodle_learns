# Measures of concentration

- [[Molarity]] vs [[Molality]]
	- *Hint: It's the denominator*
- Mass percent = $\frac{mass\ solute}{total\ mass}$
- Parts per million (ppm)
	- $\frac{1\ part}{10^6 \ parts}$
	- Often in $\frac{mg}{L}$
