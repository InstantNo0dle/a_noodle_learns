# Upper Mantle
It is **660** kilometers thick, and is mostly composed of **peridotite**, a rock richer in *iron* and *magnesium* than the [[Continental Crust|continental]] and [[Oceanic Crust]].

Two parts:
- [[Lithosphere]]
- [[Asthenosphere]]
