I learned about bit manipulation! #toresearch
- Basically just how operators can be used on 1's and 0's (binary) or true and false
- For **or**:
	- Presence of T and F = T
	- T and T = T
	- F and F = F
- For **and**:
	- Presence of T and F = F
	- T and T = T
	- F and F = T
